Package Details

Holy Land Heritage

A 7-day journey focusing on key religious and historical sites in Jerusalem, Bethlehem, and Hebron. Ideal for visitors seeking a deep introduction to the spiritual heart of Palestine.

  • Day-by-Day Itinerary

    1. Day 1: Arrival in Jerusalem, hotel check-in, and evening orientation walk.
    2. Day 2: Old City walking tour, including main historical quarters and markets.
    3. Day 3: Visit to Al-Aqsa Mosque and the Church of the Holy Sepulchre.
    4. Day 4: Day trip to Bethlehem and the Church of the Nativity.
    5. Day 5: Visit to Hebron, the Ibrahimi Mosque area, and old city markets.
    6. Day 6: Cultural activities and optional evening program.
    7. Day 7: Free time in Jerusalem and departure.

  • Included Services

    • Accommodation in 3–4 star hotels (shared rooms).
    • Daily breakfast and selected group dinners.
    • Professional local tour guide.
    • Transportation between all listed destinations.
    • Entrance fees to main sites in the program.
    • Basic travel insurance coverage.

  • Not Included

    • International flights.
    • Personal expenses and shopping.
    • Optional activities not listed in the main program.
    • Tips and gratuities.

  • Requirements

    • Valid passport.
    • Travel visa (if needed).
    • Travel insurance.
    • Minimum fitness level for walking tours.

  • Additional Information

    This tour is recommended for first-time visitors who want to experience both the historical and spiritual sides of Palestine in one structured program.
