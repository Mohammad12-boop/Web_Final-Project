Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here you can find answers to common questions about traveling with Al-Rihla Travel and visiting Palestine.

General Travel Information

Is it safe to travel with Al-Rihla Travel in Palestine?

Safety is one of our main priorities. We continuously monitor the situation, plan our routes carefully, and work with local partners and guides who know the area well. If a destination becomes unsafe, we adjust the program or offer alternatives.

Do I need a visa to enter Palestine?

Entry requirements depend on your nationality and the borders you use. Most travelers enter through neighboring countries. We recommend checking with the relevant embassy or official website before booking your trip.

What is the best time of year to visit Palestine?

Spring and autumn are usually the most comfortable seasons, with mild temperatures and pleasant weather for walking tours and outdoor visits.

Booking and Payment

How do I make a booking?

You can request a booking through our Booking/Contact form. After we receive your request, our team will contact you by email to confirm availability, details, and payment instructions.

When do I need to pay for my tour?

In most cases:

  • A deposit is required to confirm your reservation.
  • The remaining balance is usually due before the start of the tour.
  • Exact payment deadlines will be shared with you in the confirmation email.
Can I change or cancel my booking?

Changes and cancellations are possible, but may be subject to fees depending on how close to the travel date the request is made. We always try to offer flexible solutions when possible.

Tours and Packages

What is the difference between a group tour and a private tour?

The main differences are:

  • Group tour: Fixed dates, shared transportation and guide, and a set itinerary.
  • Private tour: More flexible dates and pace, with a guide and transportation arranged specifically for your group.
Can you create a custom tour for my group?

Yes. If you choose the custom tour option in our booking form, we can design a program based on your interests, dates, and budget. Custom tours are ideal for families, study groups, or organizations.

Are meals included in all packages?

Most packages include daily breakfast and some selected group meals. Details about what is included can be found in the description of each package on the Tour Packages page.

Practical Questions

What should I pack for my trip?

We recommend:

  • Comfortable walking shoes.
  • Modest clothing suitable for visiting religious sites.
  • A light jacket, hat, and sunscreen depending on the season.
  • Any personal medication you may need.
Which languages do your guides speak?

Our main tour language is English. In some cases, we can arrange guides who speak additional languages if requested in advance.
