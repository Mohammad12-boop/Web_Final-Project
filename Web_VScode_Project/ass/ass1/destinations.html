Our Destinations in Palestine

Explore some of Palestine’s most important cities and landmarks with Al-Rihla Travel. Our destinations highlight history, culture, faith, and everyday life across the homeland.

Cultural & Historical Cities

  • Jerusalem

    Jerusalem is one of the most significant cities in the world, home to important religious and historical sites. Our tours include walks through the Old City, holy places, and traditional markets.

    Tour Highlights

    • Old City Walking tour
    • Visits to key religious sites
      (like Al-Aqsa Mosque and Church of the Holy Sepulchre)
    • Free time in local markets

    Did you know? Jerusalem is home to more than 200 historical sites within the Old City walls.

  • Bethlehem

    Bethlehem is famous for the Church of the Nativity and its rich cultural traditions. Our Bethlehem tours combine historical visits with time to experience the local atmosphere.

    Tour Highlights

    • Church of the Nativity visit
    • Stops at local handicraft shops
    • Opportunities to explore the city center

    Did you know? The Church of the Nativity is one of the oldest continuously operating churches in the world.

  • Hebron

    Hebron is known for its historic old city, traditional glass and ceramics, and the Ibrahimi Mosque. Our tours introduce visitors to both the city’s heritage and its living culture.

    Tour Highlights

    • Visit to the Ibrahimi Mosque area
    • Old city walking tour
    • Traditional glass and pottery workshops

    Did you know? Hebron is famous for its traditional glassblowing workshops dating back centuries.

  • Nablus

    Nablus offers a lively old market, historic sites, and famous traditional sweets. With Al-Rihla Travel, you can experience local flavors and everyday life in this vibrant city.

    Tour Highlights

    • Old city and market tour
    • Traditional sweets tasting (such as Kunafa Nabulsia)
    • Visits to historical landmarks

    Did you know? Nablus is known as the “Sweet Capital” of Palestine because of its famous kunafa.

Nature & Unique Experiences

  • Jericho

    Jericho is one of the oldest inhabited cities in the world, located in the Jordan Valley. Our tours feature archaeological sites, scenic views, and peaceful landscapes.

    Tour Highlights

    • Visit to key archaeological sites
    • Views over the Jordan Valley
    • Optional cable car and nearby monasteries

    Did you know? Jericho is among the oldest inhabited cities on Earth, with more than 10,000 years of history.

  • Ramallah

    Ramallah is a dynamic cultural and political center with cafés, arts, and events. Our Ramallah experiences include city walks, cultural evenings, and time to enjoy local food.

    Tour Highlights

    • Guided city walk
    • Stops at local cafés and cultural centers
    • Evening programs upon request

    Did you know? Ramallah is considered a cultural hub full of arts, cafés, and modern Palestinian life.
